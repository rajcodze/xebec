Hubs normally divide the available bandwidth into the number of connections made on them. This reduces the network speed available to each system connected, also hubs are prone to network collisions
Switches do not divide the bandwidth and offer the full bandwidth to each system connected on the network. Also collisions are completely absent in a switch
10/100 mbps and 10/100/1000 mbps auto sensing switches enable automatic connections at appropriate speeds
